Brooke Bond Red Label

Brews togetherness by bringing people together over a tasty cup of tea

Brooke Bond Red Label is one of India’s largest selling tea brands. When made with a touch of your love, it gives your family that irresistible great taste that brings them together. Every cup of Red Label Tea is brewed with top-quality tea leaves to provide a tasty cup of tea.

Brooke Bond Red Label encourages coming together over a warm cup of tea with everyone around. The taste of Brooke Bond Red Label tea helps you spread warmth and cheer in your family. Brooke Bond Red Label stands for making the world a more welcoming place.
